SMS Numbers (Grizzly SMS)
Sell temporary phone numbers for WhatsApp, Telegram, etc. Customers pay from wallet — you keep the profit.
How it works
  1. You connect Grizzly and choose which services/countries to sell (below).
  2. Customer buys from Store → 📱 SMS — wallet is charged your sell price.
  3. Server rents a number from Grizzly automatically and shows it to the customer.
  4. Customer enters the number in WhatsApp/Telegram/etc., then taps Refresh code to get the SMS.
Step 1 — API connection
Not connected
Step 2 — Your profit
Grizzly charges you in credits. Sell price = (cost × USD/credit × markup) + $1 profit on every service. Example: 1 credit × $1 × 35% markup + $1 = $2.35.
Step 3 — What to sell
Click Load services & countries first, then pick a service (e.g. WhatsApp) and country (e.g. Brazil). Use Preview price to see Grizzly cost vs your sell price, then Add to catalog. Or use Add popular starter pack in Step 4.
Popular codes: wa WhatsApp · bye WhatsApp Business · fb Facebook · tg Telegram · ig Instagram · lf TikTok

Imports popular apps for all Grizzly countries (max 500 items to keep the site fast). Other apps are removed from the catalog.
