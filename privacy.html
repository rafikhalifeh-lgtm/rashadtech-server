Privacy Policy

What we collect

Email, phone number, password (stored securely hashed), optional Telegram Chat ID, purchase history, wallet transactions, and support messages.

How we use it

  • Verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send important account notifications

Cookies & local storage

We use your browser's local storage to keep you signed in, remember language preference, and cache store data for faster loading. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

Third-party services

We use trusted providers to run the service, including hosting (Netlify, Render), email delivery (Resend), optional Telegram notifications, and payment verification via Whish Money. These providers process data only as needed to deliver the service.

What we don't do

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Retention

Order and account data is kept as long as your account is active and as needed for support and legal obligations.

Your choices

You can update phone and Telegram in Profile. Contact support to request account deletion.
